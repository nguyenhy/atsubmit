# Refund Policy

Bad refund policies are infuriating. I never want you to feel ripped off. If you are unhappy with "At Submit", I want to make it right.

## The 14-Day Window

You can request a full refund within **14 days** of your initial purchase.

## Eligibility

I typically grant refunds if:
* You experienced technical issues that I couldn't resolve.
* The service did not function as described on the website.
* You were charged for a renewal but meant to cancel (and contacted me immediately).

I do **not** typically grant refunds for:
* Partial billing periods where you used the service.
* Accounts terminated due to violations of the Terms of Service.
* Repeated "trial-hopping" or abuse of this policy.

## How to request a refund

Simply send an email to **contact@atsubmit.com**. Tell me what's up, and I'll work with you to ensure you're treated fairly.
