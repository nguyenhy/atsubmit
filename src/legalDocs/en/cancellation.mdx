# Cancellation Policy

I want satisfied customers, not hostages. If you decide "At Submit" isn't for you, I make it easy to leave.

## How to cancel

Account owners can cancel at any time by following these steps:
1. Log into your account at https://atsubmit.com.
2. Navigate to **Settings**.
3. Open the **Billing** or **Subscription** section.
4. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**.

## What happens when you cancel?

* **Access:** If you have a paid subscription, you will keep your paid plan ("Pro","Plus", or so) features until the end of your current billing cycle. After that, your account will automatically downgrade to
the free plan or become inactive.
* **Data:** I will keep your data for 30 days after cancellation in case you change your mind. After 30 days, it is purged from active systems. It is fully removed from backups
within 60 days. **Content cannot be recovered once permanently deleted.**
* **Billing:** You won't be billed again once you cancel.
